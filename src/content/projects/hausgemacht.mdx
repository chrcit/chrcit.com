- hausgemacht is a techno collective based in Vienna.
- they organize parties and events in various locations
- the events are either regular techno parties or body neutrality events or sex positive events
- there is a strong focus on creating a safe space for FLINTA\* people
- there are awareness teams at the events looking after the safety of the guests
- there is a strong focus on data protection and privacy
- the internet team is responsible for all technical aspects
- this includes website, newsletter as well as custom apps made for the collective
- these apps include a ticket scanner app, an application app for the sex positive events and a passport scanner app
- Joined in April 2023
